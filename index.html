Aguas Calientes is a tourist town at the bottom of Machu Picchu that is famous for it's hot springs. Aguas Calientes, Town of Machu Picchu.

History

Machu Picchu holds so much historic meaning and means a lot to the citizens of Peru. Machu Picchu was built and inhabited starting in 1450, or at the start of the Incan empire. It was abandoned when the natives were threatened by the Spanish Conquest and the violence9. Visit National Geographic for more information

Weather

Since Machu Picchu is located in Peru it is in the Southern Hemisphere. Due to this the warm, moderate temperatures occur during the May through September months while the cold, rainier months are during the October through April.
